# Tmpest

### Find peace when you require it most.

Tmpest is a concept focused on helping people discover essential temporary spaces nearby — from accessible bathrooms to affordable short-term shelter.

The idea started from a very simple observation:

Sometimes people do not need luxury.
They just need relief, safety, comfort, or a place to stay for a while.

Tmpest aims to make those necessities easier to discover through contextual recommendations and location-aware systems.

## Vision

Tmpest is designed around two core problems:

- Helping travelers or people in unfamiliar places quickly find nearby essential facilities.
- Helping homeowners with extra rooms connect with people looking for affordable temporary shelter.

This creates a system that can support both sides:
- Visitors get accessible and low-cost options.
- Local homeowners can earn additional income from unused space.

## Planned Features

- Nearby shelter discovery
- Public and private restroom discovery
- Recommendation system for personalized results
- Context-aware suggestions
- Smart location prioritization
- Future AI/Agent integrations

## Current Status

Tmpest is currently in the architecture and research phase.

The project is actively exploring:
- recommendation systems,
- contextual ranking,
- user preference understanding,
- and intelligent discovery pipelines.

## Philosophy

Technology does not always need to solve complex futuristic problems.

Sometimes helping someone find comfort at the right moment is enough.
